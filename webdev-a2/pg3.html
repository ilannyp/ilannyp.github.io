Album Covers

Album covers, also referred to as album art, are the forefront of a commercially released studio album. The term refers to but is not limited to printed paperboard covers for records, front-facing panels of a CD package and the primary image of a digital album.

Album art has cemented itself as a necessity when an artist wishes to release an album. With the right cover, an artist can use it as a form of expression, and give the listener a peek into the world of the album. Its one of the various ways in which first impressions affect an audience's perception of the album, band or musician.

There are various ways in which an album cover is visualized. Artists can choose to put a photo of themselves, which is one of the factors that add to the perception of the band, the musician, and the music.
