Ilan's Website

Hi! Welcome to my website. My name is Ilan Firman Rahadian Mohd Syahrir Kartayasa Putra. I come from a family of 7, my parents, 2 brothers and 2 sisters. My aspiration is to be a video game developer in the future, having my own indie game studio and a hit game. Of course, being introduced to video games at 3 years old and being damn near addicted to it has greatly influenced me into pursuing this career.

About Me

Since I was raised in a family of 7, there were rarely any dull moments in my childhood with my siblings keeping me company. We were never in a right spot financially however, but I did have some entertainment at home. We owned a computer and an Xbox. The computer was my father's, and he'd download all sorts of games on it. I remember playing games like Counter-Strike and Command & Conquer. The Xbox too was his idea of "giving the kids something to do while mom and dad are gone" and it served its purpose very well. Plus, I got to play with my siblings more so that was fun. Halo, Tony Hawk, Wolfenstein. In retrospect maybe it wasn't a good idea to let kids well-under the age of 16 playing some of these but hey, we seem to have turned out fine.

Aspiration

As I stated earlier, I aspire to become a game developer in the future. Seeing games like Hollow Knight and Mass Effect 2 inspired me to strive to be someone directly involved in making works of art such as them. I will continue to work and study to make this dream of mine come true.
